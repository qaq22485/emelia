## Five Features Worth Actually Caring About

### 1. Email Warm-Up — and It's Free on Every Plan

Here's the thing nobody talks about enough: warm-up costs money elsewhere. Lemwarm alone runs $29–$49/month just for warm-up. Emelia bakes it in automatically for every mailbox you connect — even on the €37 Start plan.

When you connect an inbox, Emelia gradually ramps up activity, simulates natural email exchanges within its network, and keeps your sender reputation healthy on autopilot. No setup, no separate tool, no extra invoice.

### 2. Unlimited LinkedIn Scraping (and Bypassing the 2,500 Limit)

LinkedIn Sales Navigator caps your exports at 2,500 results per search. Emelia sidesteps this by automatically segmenting your searches — so you can extract tens of thousands of profiles without getting blocked. Just paste the Sales Navigator URL, and Emelia handles the rest, including enrichment with name, title, company, email, phone (when available), and website.

This feature alone is what makes agencies and growth teams take notice.

### 3. Multichannel Campaigns — Email + LinkedIn, Together

You can build sequences that combine email steps and LinkedIn actions (profile visits, connection requests, messages) in a single campaign flow. Add delays between steps, set exit conditions, vary your messaging with A/B tests — it all lives in one place.

The visual campaign builder isn't the most sophisticated on the market, but it gets the job done without making you feel like you need a computer science degree.

### 4. Email Finder + Phone Finder + Verifier

Emelia finds emails with a claimed accuracy of up to 80%+, verifies them (including catch-all addresses), and can surface phone numbers for direct calling. All credit-based, all pay-as-you-go. You only get charged when a result is actually found — which isn't how everyone does it.

### 5. Merged Inbox

All your campaign replies from all your connected mailboxes land in one place. No switching between accounts. For solo operators juggling multiple domains, this alone saves a meaningful chunk of time each day.
